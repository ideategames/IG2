Contribute

There is no commercial intent for IdeateGames, it is only a personal exploration, with the hope it will provide fun for many. The games will remain free, no subscription, no pay-to-play. There are costs for hosting, graphics and visual design, and perhaps content licensing or permissioning. The games are filled with images, and permission to use these is a barrier to making some topics open. Cost recovery will come through contributions, some custom content creation, and credit (referral) promotional fees.

For more information, contact ideategames@gmail.com.

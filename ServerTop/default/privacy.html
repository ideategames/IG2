Privacy Policy for Ideate Games

No personal information about players in these games will be shared with anyone who is not a developer for this game unless required by law, as part of a legal investigation.

No information is collected for this site except player name which is not required, and Facebook user ID (if you have one, and it is not required) and those are not shared unless you post your score (and player name) to the Leaderboard or in multi-player games. Then only your player name (which can be any text) is shared.

No email or text messages will be sent to you from this site for sales, marketing, or other unsolicited purposes other than what is necessary to maintain your account.

No credit card or other purchasing information is available to Ideate Games or its developers.

During the course of the games (games planned for the future), there might be opportunities for players to enter their own "observations" or other game-related data. These may be stored in the database, and become the property of the game and its owners. These player-observations might appear in some format in future versions of the games, but with no connection to or information about the player who added them.

Please direct any questions about this policy to ideategames@gmail.com.

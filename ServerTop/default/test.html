Contribute

There is no commercial intent for IdeateGames, it is only a personal exploration, with the hope it will provide fun for many. The games will remain free, no subscription, no pay-to-play. There are costs for hosting, graphics and visual design, and perhaps content licensing or permissioning. The games are filled with images, and permission to use these is the barrier to making the games open immediately. Cost recovery will come through contributions and some content and credit (referral) promotional fees. There will be a PayPal contribution box for these games, coming son.

For more information, contact ideategames@gmail.com.

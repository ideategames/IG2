Help and Scoring Rules for Clues

Touch or click on the image that matches the description.
This is a timed game, and your time ends when you have correctly identified all 16 images. Maximum points are given for doing all 16 correct identifications in one minute.
Total score is a sum of the 3 most recent games of Clues played consecutively.
Click or touch the filmstrip icon to see a filmstrip of all images at full height. Click on each large image to advance through the deck.

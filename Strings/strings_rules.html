Help and Scoring Rules for Strings

Click or touch the filmstrip icon (upper right) to see a filmstrip of all images at full height. Click on each large image to advance through the deck.
Maximum simple points (40) are awarded for Strings matching the shortest span for any 5 within the given set. All other Strings are rated against the shortest span for that set.
Total score is a sum of the 5 most recent games of Strings played consecutively.
Bonus points are given for getting the first try correct (10 pts) and for getting the correct answer fast (up to 10 pts).
Penalties (max 20) are taken for incorrect guesses (number of guesses squared), for exceeding 40 secs.

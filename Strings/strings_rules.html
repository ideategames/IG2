Help and Scoring Rules for Strings

Your goal is to create a string of 5 items in chronological or numeric order, that are as close together in time (shortest span) as you can do without making a mistake. Drag items from the pool at the bottom onto the string of positions at the top.
Dragging one item on top of another will make them swap places.
Maximum simple points (40) are awarded for Strings matching the shortest span for any 5 within the given pool. All other Strings are rated against the shortest span for that set.
Total score is a sum of the 3 most recent games of Strings played consecutively.
Bonus points are given for getting the first try correct (10 pts) and for getting the correct answer fast (up to 10 pts).
Penalties (max 10) are taken for incorrect guesses.
Click or touch the filmstrip icon to see all images at full height. Click on each large image to advance through the deck.

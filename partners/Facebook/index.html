Buckets

Buckets

Buckets is a quick game sorting objects into groups of the same category. In this game, you see a vending machine with 24 objects, and you have to select those from one category at a time. Don't select one from the wrong category!. Topics include Objects in the British Museum, Discoveries in Science, Western Literature, Western Art, Western Composers, Movies, City Latitudes. Includes a leaderboard and allows you to post achievements to Facebook.

Strings

Strings

Strings is a sequencing game, put events or topics in chronological or numerical order. Points for speed and accuracy, penalties for guesses. Topics include Objects in the British Museum, Discoveries in Science, Western Literature, Western Art, Western Composers, Movies, Nature, City Latitudes. Includes a leaderboard and allows you to post achievements to Facebook.

Collections

Collections

Collections is a many-room shortest-path object collection game. In this game, you are a gofer (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gofer) in the museum, asked to collect 4 objects from the museum and bring them to the curator for more work -- whatever it is she does. Because you are the newbie, for initiation she has only given you a set of clues to these objects, and your task is to visit the right places to find and collect objects. Topics include Objects in the British Museum, Discoveries in Science, Western Literature, Western Art, Western Composers, Movies, Nature. Allows you to post achievements to Facebook.

Clues

Clues

Clues is a quick but tricky clue-event matching game. You are given 16 images, and presented one clue at a time. Each time you touch the correct image for that clue, you are given the next, until you get them all right. Topics include Objects in the British Museum, Discoveries in Science, Western Literature, Western Art, Western Composers, Movies, Nature, City Latitudes. Includes a leaderboard and allows you to post achievements to Facebook.

Rooms

Rooms

We have a problem. A history student borrowed 11 objects to make a report on them, but then carelessly (or intentionally!) returned them to the wrong room. As the newest member of our museum staff, your task is to move them all to the right rooms, and you must finish before we open in 20 minutes! Topics include Objects in the British Museum, Discoveries in Science, Western Literature, Western Art, Western Composers, Movies, Nature.

Paths

Paths

Paths is a 2-person game of building a timeline of events. Success depends on knowing the events and strategy is in the order you play them. You play against the computer and set the skill level of the computer. Topics include Objects in the British Museum, Discoveries in Science, Western Literature, Western Art, Western Composers, Movies, City Latitudes.

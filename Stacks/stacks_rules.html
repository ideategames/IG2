Help and Scoring Rules for Rooms

We have a problem. A history student borrowed 11 objects to make a report on them, but then carelessly (or intentionally!) returned them to the wrong rooms.
As the newest member of our museum staff, your task is to move them all to the right rooms, and you must finish before we open in 20 minutes!
To move an object, drag it to cart on the left. To place it in the correct room, drag it from the cart into the room.
Rooms are arranged somewhat chronologically. All rooms have doors to other rooms, and a map is provided (button in the upper left).
Click or touch the filmstrip icon to see a filmstrip of all images at full height. Click on each large image to advance through the deck.

Help and Scoring Rules for Paths

Buckets is a category game. Your goal is to create the largest buckets you can.
Buckets are defined by whatever is common to each item. Each item belongs in several different categories, and when you click on the first item, it defines the bucket with all the categories of that item.
Each subsequent item you click on must have something in common with all the other items already in the bucket.
When there are no more items sharing something with all the other items in the bucket, that bucket will automatically close. Thus if the bucket items are still at the bottom, there is at least one more item that will fit in that bucket.
You can close the current bucket at any time and start a new bucket.
Click or touch the filmstrip icon to see all images at full height. Click on each large image to advance through the deck.
